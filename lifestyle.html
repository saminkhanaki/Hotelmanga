Lifestyle

Manga collection design, Japanese minimalism, and the rituals that turn everyday living into something worth inhabiting.

The otaku lifestyle extends well beyond what you watch and read. HotelManga's Lifestyle guides cover the physical dimension of manga fandom — how to build a collection worth displaying, how to design a living space that reflects the aesthetic values of Japanese minimalism and manga culture, and how to approach daily rituals with the same care that goes into a well-curated shelf. Practical, opinionated, and written for collectors who take the details seriously.
