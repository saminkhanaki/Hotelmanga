Last Updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to HotelManga. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we handle your information when you visit our sanctuary for manga travel and lifestyle.

2. Data We Collect

We only collect minimal data to improve your experience:

  • Analytics: Anonymous data about which manga guides (like the Lumina Method) are most popular.

Your data is used solely to:
