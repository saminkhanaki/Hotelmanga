Explore

Real-world locations from your favorite manga and anime — mapped, researched, and written for the serious fan.

Anime pilgrimage is one of the fastest-growing forms of travel in Japan — and one of the least well-documented in English. HotelManga's Explore guides map the real locations behind iconic manga and anime series: the streets, stations, shrines, and intersections that appeared on the page before they appeared on any tourist map. Each guide is built for travelers who want to stand in the exact spot, not just the general neighborhood.
