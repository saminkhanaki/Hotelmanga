Explore Destinations

Discover the real-life inspirations behind your favorite manga worlds.

Tokyo: The Sorcerer's Hub

From the neon-lit streets of Shibuya to the quiet shrines of Minato, Tokyo remains the ultimate destination for manga pilgrims. Discover the iconic locations that inspired the legendary battles of modern jujutsu sorcerers.

Osaka: Food and Spirits

Experience the vibrant energy of Dotonbori and the historic grandeur of Osaka Castle. This city offers a perfect blend of high-octane action settings and soulful culinary journeys that every manga fan dreams of.
