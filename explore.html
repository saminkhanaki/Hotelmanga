Explore Destinations

Discover the real-life inspirations behind your favorite manga worlds.

Tokyo: The Sorcerer's Hub

Have you ever dreamed of leaping out from the action-packed pages of a manga and stepping directly into the heart of Tokyo’s battles? In this article, we dissolve the boundary between imagination and reality, taking you on a two-day journey through the iconic locations of Jujutsu Kaisen. From the fateful battles of Sorcerers in the depths of the Shibuya subway to the favorite hangouts of Nobara and Gojo; this guide is a treasure map for fans of the world of Curses and modern Japanese lifestyle. Are you ready to join us and discover the magic hidden within the streets of Tokyo?

Los Angeles: Guide to the One Piece Cafe

Are you ready to step off the two-dimensional pages of manga and onto the deck of the Thousand Sunny? In this feature, we journey to the heart of Little Tokyo, Los Angeles, to discover one of the world's most electrifying anime haunts: the One Piece Cafe. From tasting "Devil Fruits" on Chef Sanji’s exclusive menu to watching the ocean through digital portholes, consider this guide your personal treasure map to an unforgettable experience in the kingdom of the Straw Hat Pirates.

Kyoto: Ancient Shadows

Step back in time through the red torii gates of Fushimi Inari. Kyoto’s timeless temples provide the perfect mystical backdrop for stories involving ancient curses, traditional clans, and historical samurai legends.

Yokohama: Port of Mystery

The seaside architecture of Minato Mirai creates a unique noir atmosphere. Known for its sophisticated port views and European-style brick buildings, Yokohama is the go-to setting for detective manga and urban thrillers.
